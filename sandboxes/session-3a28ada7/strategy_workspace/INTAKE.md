# INTAKE — Project Arete

## 1. Exact User Request
Design a super-human architecture for "Project Arete" — the ultimate application of AI to make Maxim Hilgenberg (Subject 13, 15 y/o male, creator of Telos) the best version of himself. Not coding. Not AI autonomy. Making *himself* super-human.

The system must:
- Constantly reflect on his thoughts, emotions, experiences, desires.
- Document what is wrong / needs improvement.
- Prioritize exponentially (more on that later).
- Use the strategy engine (like this one) to find super-human solutions to one chosen problem at a time.
- Optimize for: KNOWLEDGE → PRACTICE → EMOTION.
- **Crucial value:** The system must teach Maxim to solve problems HIMSELF. It must become obsolete in the most beautiful way. Each solved problem makes him stronger and less dependent on the system.
- Kickstart, not replace, his real judgement.

## 2. Hard Constraints & Forbidden Methods
- Must NOT replace Maxim's rational thinking or problem-solving ability.
- Must NOT create dependency. Every tool must be designed so that usage decreases over time.
- Must NOT be a "life script" where AI takes over decisions.
- Must NOT ignore emotional reality — emotions rule knowledge and practice.
- Must NOT be purely theoretical; must produce executable, real-life actions.
- Must NOT violate privacy or safety (e.g., no harmful self-modification).
- Must NOT assume unlimited resources (Maxim is 15, has school, limited time/energy).
