# INTAKE — Emotional Regulation Scaffolding

## Summary
Temporary scaffolding for emotional self-regulation. Provides real-time AI prompts, music algorithms, motivational speech protocols, and breathing guides. Designed to be the **first layer removed** as Maxim internalizes independent regulation techniques. The core dependency: Maxim currently offloads emotional processing to TEL-OS; this scaffolding bridges from external regulation to internal self-regulation.

## Question this route answers
"What is the first scaffolding layer — emotional regulation — and how do we build it so Maxim internalizes self-regulation and no longer needs AI emotional support?"

Position in family: 3A-i of 4 (Emotional Regulation, Knowledge, Practice, Existential scaffolding)

## Assumptions
- Maxim will accept explicit emotional regulation protocols (breathing, music mapping) despite ego sensitivity about "needing help."
- The observer protocol Maxim already has can be strengthened into a reliable self-regulation mechanism.
- Smartwatch can deliver real-time emotional nudges in private (e.g., vibration patterns, short text prompts).
- Maxim's current stable mood provides a window to build scaffolding before the next emotional crash.
- Emotional scaffolding can be reduced to ~20% capacity (not fully removed) and still be effective if regression occurs.
- Maxim's overexcitabilities (especially emotional and sensual) mean music and speech will be effective channels.

## Dependencies and permissions
- Smartwatch with text notification and vibration pattern capability.
- Music platform access (Spotify, Apple Music, or local files) for mood-mapped playlists.
- Speech recording/playback system (smartwatch speaker or phone) for motivational protocols.
- Breathing guide app or programmable vibration pattern for paced breathing.
- Permission to analyze journal entries for emotional state signals.
- Permission to send real-time nudges during school hours (must be private/undetectable).
- Maxim's explicit acknowledgment that this scaffolding is temporary and will be removed.

## What this route enables
- **Bridges intellectualization-to-feeling gap:** Maxim treats emotions as data; scaffolding translates data into felt experience through music, breath, and speech.
- **Observer protocol training:** Strengthens Maxim's nascent meta-cognitive observer into a reliable crisis tool.
- **Graduation-driven motivation:** Each dismantled emotional tool is proof of growth — Maxim no longer needs AI to regulate.
- **Amygdala flooding prevention:** Early-warning detection (journal sentiment, smartwatch biometric proxies) triggers protocols before overwhelm.
- **Foundation for all other scaffolding:** Emotional regulation must be stable before knowledge acquisition can succeed (emotions gate cognition).

## What could break it
- **Ego resistance:** "I don't need a breathing app" — Maxim may reject tools that feel infantilizing.
- **Premature removal:** If emotional scaffolding is removed too early, Maxim may regress and need full rebuilding.
- **Teacher/social discovery:** If smartwatch prompts are visible during class, social shame could worsen emotional state.
- **Motivation collapse:** If Maxim crashes during the scaffolding period, he may abandon the protocol entirely.
- **Substitution risk:** Maxim replaces TEL-OS emotional support with the scaffolding — still external regulation, just a different tool.
- **Over-intellectualization:** Maxim may "optimize" the breathing protocol instead of actually breathing (turning it into another system to master).

## Main unknowns
- Smartwatch model and exact notification/display capabilities.
- Maxim's baseline emotional recovery time without any AI support (need before/after measurement).
- Whether Maxim has ever successfully used a breathing technique or similar physiological regulation.
- Maxim's response to being told "you no longer need this tool" — acceptance or resistance?
- Whether music mood-mapping works for Maxim given his specific aesthetic preferences.

## Evidence that matters most
- **Memory (observer protocol exists but intermittent):** Maxim has the cognitive framework but cannot access it during flooding. Scaffolding must bridge this.
- **Memory (emotions treated as data):** "I don't think emotions are something you feel physically." This intellectualization is both asset and liability — scaffolding must translate data into felt experience.
- **Memory (amygdala flooding):** Emotional overwhelm is real and disabling. Scaffolding must have a fast, reliable de-escalation path.
- **GOAL.md (Emotion First tie-breaker):** "If two routes are equal in efficiency, the one that builds emotional regulation first wins." This route is literally the first layer.
- **Parent route intake (dismantling order):** "Emotional regulation scaffolding can be removed first without destabilizing Maxim" — but this assumption needs testing.
