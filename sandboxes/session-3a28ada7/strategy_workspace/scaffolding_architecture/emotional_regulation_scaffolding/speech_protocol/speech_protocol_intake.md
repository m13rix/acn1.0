# INTAKE — Speech Protocol

## Summary
Recorded motivational speech and stress-dissipation audio system. Delivers pre-recorded speeches (in Maxim's own voice or trusted voice) for de-escalation, perspective restoration, and meaning-reframing. Bridges Maxim's intellectualization with physiological regulation — uses his own philosophical vocabulary (absurdist okay, steel balls, observer protocol) to talk himself down from cognitive spirals.

## Question this route answers
"What is the concrete implementation of the Speech Protocol — how do we structure recorded motivational audio that Maxim will actually use, and how does it become obsolete as he internalizes self-talk regulation?"

Position in family: Depth 4, sub-component 2 of 4 under Emotional Regulation Scaffolding. Sibling routes: Music Ladder, Breathing App, Human Bridge.

## Assumptions
- Maxim will respond to recorded speech in his own voice (he already uses third-person self-talk: "Subject 13 is experiencing...").
- Speech content must use Maxim's own conceptual vocabulary (absurdist okay, observer, debugging protocol, steel balls) to feel authentic.
- Smartwatch or phone can play short audio clips (2-5 minutes) for discrete use.
- Maxim will resist "canned" motivational speeches — recordings must feel personal, not generic.
- Maxim already has the cognitive framework for self-observation; this protocol strengthens access during crisis.
- Recording in Maxim's own voice is the strongest option; a trusted voice (family, mentor, his own past self) is second best.
- Privacy during playback is manageable (earbuds, bathroom break, before bed).

## Dependencies and permissions
- Audio recording system (phone voice memo or higher quality).
- Permission to record Maxim's voice for playback.
- Permission to craft speech scripts that use Maxim's personal philosophical vocabulary.
- Audio playback capability (phone or smartwatch with speaker/earbuds).
- Maxim's willingness to record 5-7 short speeches (2-5 minutes each) covering: meaning collapse, rejection sensitivity, shame spiral, motivation crash, existential despair, "why bother," and celebration of progress.
- Maxim's understanding that these are temporary scaffolding recordings, not permanent crutches.

## What this route enables
- **Self-talk transformation:** Shifts Maxim from harsh internal critic ("I'm fucking ashamed of myself") to observer-compassion ("Subject 13 is experiencing catastrophic rejection sensitivity right now").
- **Meaning reframing during collapse:** A pre-recorded speech in Maxim's own voice can re-anchor him to his absurdist philosophy when meaning collapses.
- **Cognitive-to-physiological bridge:** Speech audio, combined with breathing or music, creates a multi-channel regulation experience that bypasses pure intellectualization.
- **Proof-driven motivation:** When Maxim successfully de-escalates using his own recorded voice, it becomes concrete proof of growth.
- **Voice as anchor:** The sound of his own calm voice creates a self-referential grounding effect that an external AI cannot replicate.
- **Obsolescence as skill acquisition:** Recording is used less as Maxim internalizes the phrases and can generate them in real-time.

## What could break it
- **Cringe/shame barrier:** Maxim may find listening to his own voice uncomfortable or "cringe" and avoid using the recordings.
- **Over-production:** Maxim might spend more time perfecting the recordings than actually using them.
- **Script alienation:** If the speech scripts feel like AI-generated therapy-speak rather than his authentic voice, he will reject them.
- **Crisis inaccessibility:** During actual amygdala flooding, Maxim may not remember or be able to access the recordings.
- **Premature deletion:** Maxim may delete recordings once he feels better, removing the safety net.
- **Single-voice fatigue:** Repetition of the same recordings may reduce effectiveness over time (need rotation/update).

## Main unknowns
1. Maxim's response to hearing his own recorded voice — comfort or cringe?
2. Whether Maxim can reliably access recordings during emotional flooding.
3. How many recordings are needed to avoid fatigue without overwhelming inventory.
4. The optimal length (2-5 minutes is hypothesized; needs testing).
5. Whether third-person ("Subject 13") or first-person ("I") voice is more effective.

## Evidence that matters most
- **Memory (observer protocol):** Maxim already uses third-person self-talk with observer framing. This is a pre-existing skill to strengthen.
- **Memory (self-criticism):** "I'm fucking ashamed of myself" — the harsh internal critic is well-documented. Speech protocol directly counters this.
- **Memory (philosophical vocabulary):** Maxim's absurdist framework, steel balls metaphor, and "okay" acceptance are powerful meaning-anchors.
- **Web research (self-talk interventions):** Cognitive reappraisal and compassionate self-talk are evidence-based for emotional regulation.
- **Web research (humming breath/vocal stimulation):** Vibration from voiced speech stimulates vagus nerve — even if Maxim only listens to the audio, the vocal qualities can trigger physiological regulation.
- **Parent route intake (intellectualization):** Speech protocol bridges intellectual framework with felt experience, addressing the core gap identified in the parent route.
