2. Whether Maxim's physiological arousal response aligns with standard BPM-emotion mappings.
3. Whether Maxim experiences all 6 mood categories, or if his emotional landscape is better described by fewer or different states.
4. Whether Maxim will engage with the matrix as a reference tool or ignore it.

## Confidence
**High (82%)** — Strong evidence base, excellent psychological fit, and zero-risk implementation. Main uncertainty is personalization (music preferences), which is addressable in Week 1.

## Why keep is justified
1. **Foundational necessity:** Without the Mood-BPM Matrix, the other three parameter packages have no data to operate on. It is the vocabulary of the Music Ladder.
2. **Best evidence base:** BPM-emotion mappings are well-established in music therapy literature with cross-cultural validation.
3. **Perfect psychological fit:** Structured, data-driven approach matches Maxim's systems-thinking cognitive style.
4. **Zero friction:** This is a reference document, not a new behavior. It supports rather than demands.
5. **Enables all other packages:** Transition Protocol, Delivery Loop, and Fade Schedule all depend on this matrix for their operations.
6. **Information value:** Building the matrix reveals Maxim's emotional vocabulary and music associations — essential data for all Project Arete scaffolding.
