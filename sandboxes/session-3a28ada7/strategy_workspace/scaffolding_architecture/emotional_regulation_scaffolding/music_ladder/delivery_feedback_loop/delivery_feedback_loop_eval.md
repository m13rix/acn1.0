## What-if checks

**What if Maxim finds mood check-ins intrusive?**
→ Simplify to a single post-listening question: "Did this help?" (yes/no). Or embed check-in within the Journaling Protocol (sibling route at Practice Scaffolding depth 4).

**What if the smartwatch model lacks music control?**
→ Phone delivery is the MVP fallback. A Telegram message with a Spotify link is sufficient.

**What if this route is bad as a final route but excellent as an information-gathering opener?**
→ Moderate information value. The Mood-BPM Matrix reveals more about Maxim's emotional landscape. The feedback loop primarily reveals song effectiveness, which is secondary.

**What if this route works and completely reframes the problem?**
→ Best case: Maxim becomes so self-aware through check-ins that he no longer needs playlists — he just knows what to listen to. This accelerates obsolescence.

## Confidence
**Moderate (65%)** — Technical feasibility is high, but psychological fit and execution likelihood are uncertain. Risk of "another tracking system" fatigue.

## Why kill is justified
1. **Lower necessity:** The Music Ladder can function with simple playlist links and no formal feedback system. Mood-BPM Matrix + free listening is the core intervention.
2. **Tracking fatigue risk:** Maxim is already managing multiple Project Arete protocols. Adding a formal check-in system increases cognitive load.
3. **Absorbed by other routes:** The Journaling Protocol (Practice Scaffolding, depth 4) already includes daily emotional logging. Mood check-ins can be embedded there rather than as a standalone system.
4. **Information value is secondary:** Song effectiveness data is useful for matrix refinement, but the matrix can be refined through conversation rather than formal ratings.
5. **Higher friction than value:** The delivery and feedback loop adds operational complexity without clear proportional benefit.

## Why keep as fallback
If Mood-BPM Matrix alone proves insufficient (Maxim listens but does not notice mood shifts), the Delivery & Feedback Loop provides the measurement infrastructure to diagnose and fix the problem.
