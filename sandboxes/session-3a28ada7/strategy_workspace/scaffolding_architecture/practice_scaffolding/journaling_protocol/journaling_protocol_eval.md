## What-if checks

**What if Maxim already journals consistently?**
→ Score increases. Shift from "building consistency" to "deepening quality." Use prompts for emotional granularity and self-compassion rather than frequency.

**What if journaling becomes obsessive?**
→ This is the intellectualization loop (GOAL.md failure state). Mitigation: daily word limit (max 1 page), time limit (5 min max), and explicit "done is better than perfect" framing.

**What if Maxim refuses structured prompts?**
→ Alternative: one open question per day ("What's on your mind?"). Still keeps the consistency metric. Structure can be introduced gradually.

**What if this works and Maxim becomes genuinely independent?**
→ Best case: journaling becomes a permanent self-care tool, not a scaffold. The AI component retires, but Maxim continues journaling independently. This is the exact obsolescence outcome.

5. **Fastest feedback loop:** Consistency is measurable within the first week. Adjustments can be made immediately.
6. **Foundation for other siblings:** Journaling provides the reflective component that makes the Visible-Action Planner and Submission Rhythm Tracker more effective (reviewing what worked, what didn't).
