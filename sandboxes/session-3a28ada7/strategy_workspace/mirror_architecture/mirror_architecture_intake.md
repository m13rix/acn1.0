# INTAKE — Mirror Architecture

## Summary
Build Project Arete as a reflective surface. It does not give answers; it surfaces patterns in Maxim's own data (journal entries, emotional logs, conversation transcripts) and asks Maxim to interpret them. Data streams include journaling pattern analysis, conversation transcript analysis, biometric proxies, and behavioral pattern detection. The AI holds up a mirror; Maxim does the seeing.

## Question This Route Answers
"What is the highest-level structural approach to building Project Arete?"
Position in family: 2C of 5 (Scaffolding, Teacher, Mirror, Ecosystem, Crisis-Intervention)

## Assumptions
- Maxim will actually journal consistently (>5 days/week).
- Journal data is rich enough for meaningful pattern analysis.
- Transcription system is ready soon enough for conversation analysis.
- Biometric data (sleep, heart rate) is available from smartwatch.
- Maxim wants to see his own patterns rather than receive guidance.

## Dependencies and Permissions
- **Critical:** Transcription system for conversation analysis (timeline uncertain — "almost ready")
- Journaling system with semantic analysis capability
- Smartwatch with biometric sensors (sleep, heart rate)
- Behavioral logging capability (phone usage, app switching)
- Data analysis pipeline for pattern detection and visualization
- Permission to analyze all journal entries
- Permission to analyze conversations (privacy-sensitive)
- Permission to track behavioral patterns (may feel invasive)

## What This Route Enables
- **Non-directive growth:** No dependency risk — the AI doesn't solve problems, it reflects them.
- **Low burden:** Minimal daily time commitment; mostly passive data collection.
- **Pattern surfacing:** Could surface patterns Maxim misses due to emotional proximity (e.g., "you always journal about 14 when your sleep drops").
- **Observer protocol formalization:** Takes Maxim's nascent observer skill and makes it systematic.
- **Self-discovery:** Maxim interprets his own data, building ownership of insights.

## What Could Break It
- **Diminishing returns:** Maxim already has exceptional metacognition. He may not learn new patterns, only confirm what he already knows.
- **Intellectualization loop risk:** Could fuel the exact failure state: "Project Arete becomes another system to optimize, avoiding real emotional work" (GOAL.md).
- **Transcription delay:** If transcription is months away, this architecture is severely limited.
- **Journaling inconsistency:** If Maxim doesn't journal (target: >5 days/week), the mirror has no data.
- **Confirmation bias:** Mirror surfaces patterns Maxim already suspects, reinforcing rather than challenging.
- **Privacy concerns:** Biometric and behavioral tracking may feel invasive to a 15-year-old.
- **Passive format:** Maxim's motivation engine is proof, not reflection. He may lose interest without active challenges.

## Main Unknowns
- Transcription system readiness (days, weeks, or months?)
- Smartwatch biometric capabilities (heart rate, sleep tracking available?)
- Maxim's actual willingness to journal consistently
- Whether Maxim finds pattern visualizations engaging or boring
- Whether self-discovered insights have more or less impact than guided insights

## Evidence That Matters Most
- **Memory (self-awareness):** "Surprising amount of self-observation" — suggests mirror may have diminishing returns.
- **Memory (observer protocol development):** Maxim already has an observer. Mirror formalizes what exists rather than creating new capability.
- **GOAL.md §3 (Exclusions):** "The system does not need to solve Maxim's problems for him — only teach him to solve them himself." Mirror's non-directive approach aligns with this, but Mirror doesn't teach — it only reflects.
- **DEPTH_PLAN.md note:** Mirror is listed as a valid family but with the weakest initial evidence.
