# INTAKE — Crisis-Intervention Architecture

## Summary
Build Project Arete primarily as an emotional safety net. The first priority is preventing meaning collapse and suicidal ideation; all other features are secondary. Multi-layered safety architecture: early warning system (signals predicting meaning collapse), real-time de-escalation protocol (music, speech, breathing, human contact), post-crisis integration protocol (learning without shame), and professional escalation pathway (therapist/crisis line).

## Question This Route Answers

Position in family: 2E of 5 (Scaffolding, Teacher, Mirror, Ecosystem, Crisis-Intervention)

## Assumptions
- Suicidal ideation risk is latent even during current stable period.
- Early warning signals can be reliably detected before crisis.
- Maxim will use de-escalation protocols when triggered (not resist them).
- Maxim has at least one trusted human for escalation contact.
- No therapist is currently available (memory search confirmed), so escalation pathway must include crisis lines and family.
- Smartwatch can detect behavioral warning signals.

## Dependencies and Permissions
- Smartwatch for continuous monitoring and real-time alerts
- Journaling for sentiment/tone analysis (early warning)
- Music library with emotional state mapping
- Speech recording/playback system for motivational messages
- Breathing guide (app or smartwatch capability)
- Trusted human contact list for escalation (family, friends)
- Crisis line numbers (local to Maxim's region in Russia)
- Permission to monitor emotional signals continuously
- Permission to trigger interventions automatically
- Privacy/ethics framework for monitoring

## What This Route Enables
- **Safety first:** Directly addresses the highest-risk domain (suicidal ideation).
- **Early detection:** Catches meaning collapse before it becomes crisis.
- **Rapid recovery:** Real-time de-escalation reduces crash duration (target: <24 hours).
- **Post-crisis learning:** Each crisis becomes a learning opportunity, not a shame spiral.
- **Professional bridge:** When needed, routes to professional help.
- **Emotion First alignment:** GOAL.md tie-breaker #2 favors emotional regulation first.

## What Could Break It
- **Currently stable:** Maxim's latest state is "I just feel happy." Crisis-only architecture underutilizes this stable window for growth.
- **Does not address knowledge/practice pillars:** GOAL.md requires autonomous problem-solving and knowledge-practice integration. Crisis alone doesn't build these.
- **Dependency risk:** If crisis tools are the only tools, Maxim may become dependent on them for emotional regulation rather than building internal capacity.
- **False positives:** Early warning system may trigger unnecessarily, causing alarm fatigue.
- **False negatives:** System may miss subtle warning signs, especially if Maxim hides distress.
- **Privacy concerns:** Continuous monitoring of a 15-year-old may feel invasive.
- **Professional gap:** No therapist currently available — escalation pathway is limited.

## Main Unknowns
- Current stability duration — how long will the positive window last before next potential crisis?
- Whether early warning signals are detectable with available data (journaling, smartwatch)
- Which de-escalation methods work best for Maxim (music, speech, breathing, human contact)
- Whether Maxim has a trusted human willing to be on crisis call
- Crisis line availability in Maxim's region (Russia)
- Long-term emotional regulation skill development timeline
