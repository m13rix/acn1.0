## What-if checks

**What if Maxim responds brilliantly to Socratic questioning?**
→ Then Teacher content becomes the *content* of a Scaffolding layer. Teacher is a pedagogical method, not a structural architecture. Scaffolding can deliver Socratic teaching as a temporary layer.

**What if stronger preparation makes it viable?**
→ Preparation doesn't change the structural issue: Teacher model has no built-in obsolescence mechanism. It can be *made* to have one, but then it's just Scaffolding with Teacher content.

**What if it is bad as a final route but excellent as an information-gathering opener?**
→ This is its best role. Teacher methods (Socratic questioning, deliberate practice) should be *components* of the winning architecture, not the architecture itself.

**What if this route works and completely reframes the problem?**
→ If it works, Maxim becomes a self-directed learner. But the structural question is "how do we build the system?" not "what content does it deliver?" Teacher answers the latter.

## Main unknowns
1. Whether Maxim's patience for Socratic questioning is sufficient during low-motivation phases
2. Which pedagogical sub-family resonates most (Socratic, deliberate practice, constructionist, mentorship)
3. Whether Teacher model alone can achieve obsolescence (it can't — needs Scaffolding meta-layer)

## Confidence
**Medium (70%)** — Strong psychological fit but weak structural alignment with the core question. Teacher is a *content* model, not a *structural* model.

## Why kill is justified
1. **Not a structural answer:** The question is "what is the highest-level structural approach?" Teacher is a pedagogical approach — it describes *how* content is delivered, not *how the system is built*.
2. **No obsolescence mechanism:** GOAL.md's #1 tie-breaker is obsolescence. Teacher model has no built-in mechanism to make itself unnecessary. A teacher can teach forever.
3. **Risk of intellectualization:** Maxim's pattern of "identify system → model as optimization → design solution → hit ambiguity → crash" could be *reinforced* by a Teacher model that keeps him in system-thinking mode without emotional grounding.
4. **Slower than Scaffolding:** Teacher model requires time for Socratic exploration. Scaffolding can deliver faster wins (e.g., music ladder producing results in Week 1-2).
5. **Composable, not standalone:** Teacher methods are valuable but should be *embedded* within a Scaffolding architecture. Socratic questioning becomes a scaffolding layer that gets removed when Maxim self-questions.

## What to salvage
- Socratic method → embed as a scaffolding layer
- Deliberate practice framework → embed as a scaffolding layer
- Constructionist learning → embed as a scaffolding layer
- Mentorship simulation → embed as a scaffolding layer
