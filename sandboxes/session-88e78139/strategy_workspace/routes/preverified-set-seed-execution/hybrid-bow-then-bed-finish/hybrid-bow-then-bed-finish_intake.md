# INTAKE - Hybrid Bow Then Bed Finish

## Exact route question
Inside a pre-verified set-seed run, should the dragon be finished mostly with bow damage and only a small number of controlled bed detonations at the best low-risk windows?

## Why this route exists
- It keeps most of the bed route's speed.
- It uses bow damage as a safer default while the player positions and waits for the best perch window.
- It is a middle ground between raw speed and self-kill avoidance.

## Assumptions
- The player can do normal bow shots comfortably.
- The player can execute at least one or two controlled bed placements when the dragon is centered or perched.
- The player prefers a finish that tolerates one bad window without instantly collapsing the run.

## Dependencies
- A working bow.
- Enough arrows / durability.
- Basic bed handling under controlled conditions.

## What could break it
- The player over-relies on bow damage and loses the time advantage.
- The bed placement still goes wrong in the final burst window.
- The player is slow to switch between bow and bed behavior.

## Information still needed
- Whether the user can do a controlled bed placement at least once in a drill.
- Whether a mostly-bow finish still leaves the run competitive enough.
- Whether this is the best compromise for a first stable clear.
