# INTAKE - Bow and Melee Finish

## Exact route question
Should the dragon be finished without beds, using bow damage during flight and melee on perch for a simple, low-risk but slower End finish?

## Why this route exists
- It is the simplest no-bed End finish family.
- It avoids self-kill risk from bed explosions.
- It can be a fallback if the player is not ready for bed timing.

## Assumptions
- The player has enough arrows or bow durability to sustain the fight.
- The player can survive the dragon's attacks while taking a longer fight.
- The player can hit the dragon reliably enough to avoid a total stall.

## Dependencies
- A usable bow.
- Basic aiming and spacing.
- Enough healing / food to survive the longer engagement.

## What could break it
- The fight runs long enough that more mistakes accumulate.
- The player runs low on arrows or loses shot opportunities.
- The slower kill time makes the route less competitive than a bed-based finish.

## Information still needed
- Whether the user can handle a longer End fight safely.
- Whether the extra time is acceptable compared with the bed variants.
- Whether this should only remain as a fallback if bed tests fail.
