# INTAKE - Fast Bed Finish

## Exact route question
Should the dragon be finished with the fastest possible bed detonation pattern, minimizing safety steps and trusting the player to time the blasts cleanly?

## Why this route exists
- Beds are the highest-damage End finisher.
- The route aims to minimize the number of damage windows and the time spent in the dragon fight.
- It is the raw-speed version of the End finish.

## Assumptions
- The player can already recognize perch timing quickly.
- The player can place and detonate beds without panic.
- The player can tolerate a low margin for error.

## Dependencies
- Precise bed placement.
- Strong enough gear to survive minor mistakes.
- Good emotional control under time pressure.

## What could break it
- A single bad bed placement kills the player.
- The player hesitates and loses the speed advantage.
- The player has not yet proven enough End-fight timing skill.

## Information still needed
- Whether the user can chain bed detonations safely in a short drill.
- Whether the user can recover from a near-miss without losing the run.
- Whether raw speed is worth the likely reset risk for this user.
