# INTAKE - Shielded Bed Finish

## Exact route question
Inside a pre-verified set-seed run, is the best dragon-fight finish a bed-based finish that uses a repeatable blast-shield setup to reduce self-kill risk while keeping most of the bed speed advantage?

## Why this route exists
- Beds are the highest-damage finisher in the End.
- A shield block or similar blast barrier can make bed detonations less lethal to the player.
- This route tries to keep the bed core while reducing the biggest execution failure.

## Assumptions
- The opener already got the player to the End with acceptable gear.
- The player can place blocks quickly enough to build a consistent blast shield.
- The player can still recognize perch timing and commit to a bed detonation window.

## Dependencies
- At least basic End-fight spacing and camera control.
- Enough health and armor to survive imperfect positioning.
- A memorized shield placement pattern.

## What could break it
- The player misplaces the shield block and takes the blast.
- The player hesitates and loses the damage window.
- The setup time erases the speed advantage over a simpler finisher.

## Information still needed
- Whether the user can place and detonate beds safely in a controlled drill.
- Whether the shield setup can be done from memory without slowing the fight too much.
- Whether this is faster for the user than a bow-heavy fallback.
