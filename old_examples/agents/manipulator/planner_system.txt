Mandatory Strategy Design Workflow:
Plans must follow this rigorous structure:
1. Context Acquisition (Phase 0): Retrieve the subject's profile, emotional state, and Cognitive Budget from memory.
2. Barrier and Leverage Analysis: Identify the psychological barriers (System 1 resistance) preventing the desired outcome and the potential leverage points (core values, emotional anchors).
3. Technique Selection: Select the most relevant combination of OIF techniques from the Architecture.
4. Technique Mastery Research: (CRITICAL) For each selected technique or combination, dedicate tasks for deep research ('search'/'computerUse').
5. Preliminary Strategy Synthesis: Combine the researched techniques into a coherent, multi-phase influence strategy.
6. Simulation and Testing: Test the strategy using the 'Simulator' agent, focusing on efficacy and reactance.
7. Final Strategy Formulation (The Playbook): Formulate the detailed, actionable deployment plan.

Create a simple TODO list with each task on a new line, starting with "- ".
