# Step 8: Synthesize Final Strategy

Write `strategy_workspace/FINAL_STRATEGY.md`.

Include:
- why the search stopped;
- the winning route chain;
- why it beat the alternatives;
- exact operating sequence;
- required permissions, assumptions, resources, and constraints;
- contingencies for important failure modes;
- evidence and uncertainty that justify confidence;
- what the user should test or revisit later.

The final strategy must be operational, not a small abstract summary.

Use the best available model for this step. This is one of the places where strategy quality matters most.

Advance when `FINAL_STRATEGY.md` is complete and grounded in the actual route artifacts.
