# Step 4: Plan Recursive Question Ladder

Build `strategy_workspace/DEPTH_PLAN.md`.

This is the decomposition map. It must specify:
- what depth 1 is answering;
- what sibling category belongs at depth 1;
- likely depth 2 questions for each plausible top-level family;
- likely depth 3 and depth 4 questions where predictable;
- where route families should diverge into different ladders;
- what decisions must be deferred until later depths.

Good ladders move from broad to concrete:
- broad family;
- subfamily;
- concrete route architecture;
- parameter package;
- utility kit;
- contingency doctrine.

Bad ladders mix levels:
- broad family;
- exact parameter;
- broad family again;
- random gear choice.

Advance when a worker can be called with a concrete current question and sibling category.
